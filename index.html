How to use Joanie
01
Monitor media performance
Open Media Match for real-time Revenue/Call, GP margin, QM fill rate, and client CPA vs target. WTD, MTD, and QTD period switching built in.
02
Right-price your publishers
Open QM Right Pricing, select the active season, and hit Pull Notion. MPQS scores, bids, and RP actions refresh live from your 51-publisher Notion database.
03
Manage your BD pipeline
Open the BD Command Center to track pipeline health, client utilization, ICP scoring, and AEP critical path. 700-agent AEP capacity is the lead pitch for Q3.
04
Update with Claude
Paste fresh publisher data or BD updates into a Joanie conversation. Claude calculates outputs, updates Notion, and the dashboards reflect the changes on next pull.

How to use Joanie
01
Update publisher data
Paste fresh publisher performance data (CPA, CR, Effectuation, RDE) into a Joanie conversation each period. Claude calculates RP outputs and syncs to Notion automatically.
02
Pull live from Notion
Open the QM Right Pricing dashboard, select the active season (SEP/OEP/AEP), and hit Pull Notion. Data refreshes instantly from the live database.
03
Generate partner scorecards
Use the Partner View tab to pull any publisher's scorecard — MPQS grade, KPIs vs targets, suggested bid, and RP action. Share directly with media partners.
04
Push RP outputs back
After reviewing bids and actions in the dashboard, hit Push to Notion to write MPQS grades, RP actions, and suggested bids back to the Notion database for record-keeping.
